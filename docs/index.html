It's Just a Game

A private, end-to-end encrypted party game for friends and family. Six mini games, one wheel — and no servers that can read anything.

How it works

1

One person starts a game, names the players, and sends each of them their own invite link — by iMessage, WhatsApp, however you like.

2

Everyone joins with a tap. A spinning wheel picks who chooses each round's mini game. Win rounds; first to the target takes the game.

3

Rematch is one tap — the next game starts by itself for the whole crew, no new links needed.

The games

🧭

Sense of Direction

A place appears — point your arrow at it from where you're standing. GPS knows the truth. Closest bearing takes the point.

🙈

Hide & Seek

Secretly pick a square on the grid, then take turns searching. The last player left hidden wins the round.

🃏

Higher or Lower

A card is dealt. Call the next one higher or lower — wrong and you're out. Last one standing scores.

🔁

Repeat After Me

Watch the pads flash, then tap the sequence back from memory. It grows every turn. One slip and you're out.

⚡

Lightning

Wait for it… TAP! Reaction measured to the millisecond. Jump early and it's a false start.

📍

Put Your Finger On It

A bare map with no names, a place to find, 15 seconds. Drop your pin closest to its capital.

Privacy

The whole game is end-to-end encrypted.

  • No accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking.
  • The encryption key travels only inside your invite link — servers store unreadable ciphertext.
  • Contacts and locations are used on your device, shared only with your own game's players.
